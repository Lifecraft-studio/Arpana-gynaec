Dr. Arpana Jain

Dr. Arpana Jain is a practicing Obstetrician, Gynaecologist, and Laparoscopic Surgeon. She has extensive experience in her field, having worked both in India and abroad for over 15 years. A graduate of Gajaraja Medical College, Gwalior, M.P., she completed her post-graduation in Gynaecology and Obstetrics from Shyam Shah Medical College, Rewa. Her reputation precedes her in providing the highest standard of clinical care, whether it be medical or surgical management of gynaecological issues and laparoscopic conditions.

Over time, while practicing, she developed a special interest in the fields of Gynae Endoscopy and Reproductive Medicine, leading her to obtain fellowships in both areas. Apart from practicing at her clinic, Dr. Jain ensures a thorough understanding of each case by reviewing a woman’s medical history, including familial history. During consultations and treatments, patients who lack knowledge about their biological history are tested for any genetic predispositions.

Each patient benefits from her “team” approach, receiving personalized, compassionate, and individualized care combined with cutting-edge treatments and protocols.

Gynecology

Diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of female reproductive system disorders, including menstrual disorders, fibroid uterus, endometriosis, and ovarian cysts. Comprehensive care in gynecology also encompasses urogynaecology, addressing issues such as urinary incontinence and pelvic floor disorders, as well as menopause management.

Obstretics

Dr. Jain offers comprehensive prenatal, antenatal, and postpartum care, with a particular focus on managing high-risk pregnancies. Her services also include pre-marital and pre-pregnancy counseling, helping couples understand reproductive health and prepare for a healthy pregnancy.

Infetility Specialist

Dr. Jain conducts thorough evaluations for both male and female fertility, diagnosing underlying issues and implementing effective treatment plans. Her infertility treatments include induction of ovulation, follicular tracking, and intrauterine inseminations, aiding couples in their journey to conception.

Laparoscopic Surgeon

Dr. Jain is proficient in laparoscopic and hysteroscopic surgeries, utilizing these minimally invasive techniques to correct various gynecological conditions. These endoscopic corrective surgeries provide patients with quicker recovery times and less postoperative pain compared to traditional open surgeries.
